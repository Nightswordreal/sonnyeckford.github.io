Pwnagotchi

Mobile WiFi Auditing Build

Introduction

The Pwnagotchi is a popular A2C-based "AI" for WiFi auditing, but most builds rely on bulky battery hats or standard cases that sacrifice portability. For this project, my goal was to push the limits of the form factor. I set out to create a truly pocket-sized, slim-profile Pwnagotchi by physically modifying the hardware, integrating a dedicated power source, and designing a custom-fitted enclosure from the ground up to ensure it was both functional and rugged for mobile use.
